Hello!

I am a digital & media preservationist, and technologist based in Chicago.My work focuses on integrating and migrating museum and archival collections, designing open-source data and media workflows for cultural heritage preservation, and preserving web-based works.

Currently, I am the Digital Preservation Specialist the University of Chicago Library where I contribute to UChicagoNode, an integrated digital collections platform bringing together digital collections across the University.

Previously, I worked in digital preservation, film restoration, and media preservation at the Asian Film Archive in Singapore, J. Paul Getty Trust in Los Angeles, and CUNY TV in New York City. I have also completed internships at the Bay Area Video Coalition (BAVC), Cineric Inc., CUNY TV, Internet Archive, the New York Public Library (NYPL), and Prelinger Archives during my graduate studies in Moving Image Archiving and Preservation (MIAP) at the Tisch School of the Arts, New York University.

Originally from sunny Singapore, I enjoy grocery shopping, sailing, and hiking in my free time.
