Hello!

I am a digital archivist and media preservationist based in New York City. My research explores migration for audiovisual collections, open-source media workflows, preservation of software- and web-based art, and web3 technologies for audiovisual preservation.

I am the Research Fellow at the Digital Preservation Outreach & Education Network (DPOE-N) and Audio Preservation Intern at CUNY TV. Prior to this, I worked in digital preservation, film restoration, and media digitization at the Asian Film Archive in Singapore. I am currently pursuing an M.A. in Moving Image Archiving and Preservation (MIAP) at the Martin Scorsese Department of Cinema Studies, New York University.

I grew up in Singapore and enjoy frequenting supermarkets in my free time.
