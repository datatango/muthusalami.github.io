Hello!

I am a digital archivist and media preservationist based in New York City. My research explores migration for audiovisual collections, open-source media workflows, preservation of software- and web-based art, and web3 technologies for audiovisual preservation.

I am currently the Audio Preservation Intern at CUNY TV and a recent graduate of the Moving Image Archiving & Preservation (MIAP) Program at New York University, Tisch School of the Arts. Before this, I worked in digital preservation, film restoration, and media digitization at the Asian Film Archive in Singapore.

I grew up in sunny Singapore and enjoy frequenting supermarkets in my free time.
