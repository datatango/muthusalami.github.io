Matthew Yang

Hello! I am a media preservationist and digital archivist based in New York City. My research explores migration for audiovisual collections, open-source media workflows, presevation of software and web-based art, and web3 technologies for audiovisual preservation.

I am the Research Fellow at the Digital Preservation Outreach & Education Network (DPOE-N) and Film Restoration Intern at Cineric Inc. Before this, I worked in film restoration, media digitization, and digital preservation at the Asian Film Archive in Singapore. I am currently pursuing an M.A. in Moving Image Archiving and Preservation (MIAP) at the Martin Scorsese Department of Cinema Studies, New York University.

I grew up in Singapore and enjoy frequenting supermarkets in my free time.
