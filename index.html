Hello!

I am a digital archivist, media preservationist & technologist based in Los Angeles. My work focuses on the integration & migration of museum & archival collections, developing open-source data & media workflows, and preserving software & web-based works.

Currently, I am the Collection & Content Management Systems Graduate Intern at the J. Paul Getty Trust, where I develop digital preservation, video digitization, and web archiving workflows for Getty Digital (GDI).

Prior to this, I worked in digital preservation, film restoration, and media preservation at the Asian Film Archive in Singapore and CUNY TV in New York City. I have also completed internships at the Bay Area Video Coalition (BAVC), Cineric Inc., CUNY TV, the New York Public Library, and Prelinger Archives during my graduate studies in Moving Image Archiving and Preservation (MIAP) at the Tisch School of the Arts, New York University.

Originally from sunny Singapore, I enjoy walking down supermarket aisles in my free time.
