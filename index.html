Hello!

I am a digital archivist and media preservationist based in Los Angeles. My work focuses on the migration of audiovisual collections, developing open-source media workflows, and preserving software & web-based works.

Currently, I am the Collection & Content Management Systems Graduate Fellow at the J. Paul Getty Trust, where I develop digital preservation, video digitization, and web archiving workflows for Getty Digital. Before this, I worked in digital preservation, film restoration, and media preservation at the Asian Film Archive in Singapore & CUNY TV in New York City. I also completed internships at the Bay Area Video Coalition (BAVC), Cineric Inc., CUNY TV, the New York Public Library, and Prelinger Archives during my graduate studies in Moving Image Archiving and Preservation (MIAP) at New York University.

Originally from sunny Singapore, I enjoy walking down supermarket aisles in my free time.
